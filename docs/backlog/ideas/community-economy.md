# Community Economy — Ideas Pool

Monetization, brand partnerships, and marketplace ideas. All require payment infrastructure (see `../next/payments-stripe.md`) to be built first.

---

## Brand & Sponsorship

| Idea | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Brand-sponsored challenges | Brands (Adidas, Nike) sponsor monthly community challenges with prizes |
| Brand analytics dashboard | Sponsor can see reach, engagement, and demographics of their challenge |
| Virtual currency / RunPoints | Earn points for logging runs, engaging in community — redeem for rewards |
| Rewards marketplace | Redeem RunPoints for: discounts, event entries, gear |

---

## Community Revenue

| Idea | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Digital collectibles / NFTs | Commemorative race finish digital collectibles (Proof-of-Completion) |
| Premium community subscriptions | Monthly fee for exclusive content, coaching plans, premium gear access |
| Community merchandise store | Integrated Stripe Connect store for branded gear sales |
| Sponsored challenges leaderboard | Paid placement for brand challenge on the home page |

---

## Coaching Economy

| Idea | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Paid coaching plans | Coaches sell training programs directly through RunHub |
| 1-on-1 coaching sessions | Book a video call with a RunHub-certified coach |
| Coach certification | RunHub coach badge after completing verification |
| Revenue sharing | Platform takes 15% of coach sales, 85% to coach (via Stripe Connect) |
