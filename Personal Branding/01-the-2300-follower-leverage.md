# The 2,300 Follower Advantage

Starting an app from zero is incredibly difficult. Starting with 2,300 followers changes the math completely. You already have a warm audience to launch to.

## 1. The Reactivation Campaign
If you haven't posted in a while, the algorithm has deprioritized your account. You need to "wake up" your followers before launching the app.

- **Step 1:** Post an Instagram Reel highlighting the problem with current running apps (e.g., Strava is getting expensive, the maps are boring).
- **Step 2:** Use a poll on your Instagram Story: *"I'm building a new running app. Do you care more about A) Better privacy or B) Cooler looking maps?"* Interacting with the poll trains the algorithm to show them your next story.

## 2. Leveraging the Export Studio (The Sandbox)
You built the Export Studio so users can share their runs. But **right now, you are your own best user.**
- Manually use your Angular app on your computer to generate highly aesthetic 9:16 canvases using the "Typography Poster" or "Aesthetic Text Cloud" templates.
- Save them, and post them directly to your 2,300 followers to prove that the product actually works and looks gorgeous. 

*Mindset:* Do not tell people you are building an Export Studio. **Show** them the stunning output of the Export Studio every single day on your story. Let them ask, *"How did you make this graphic?"*
