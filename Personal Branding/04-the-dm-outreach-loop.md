# The Direct Message (DM) Outreach Loop

Your 2,300 followers are great, but the goal is to reach 10,000+. To do that, you need to use Direct Messages as a surgical marketing tool.

## The "I Made This For You" Playbook
As a developer, you have a massive advantage: you are building custom software.

1. Browse Instagram for a running club or a prominent local runner.
2. Find a photo of a run they did.
3. Feed that photo through your Export Studio to create a stunning typography poster.
4. DM them the raw image directly:
   > *"Hey [Name]! I'm a solo developer building a new running app and I loved your run today. I ran your photo through the custom graphics engine I'm building. Feel free to post this on your story if you like it, no pressure! Keep crushing it.*"

**The Result:** 
- 90% of them will say "Wow, this looks amazing, thank you!"
- 70% of them will post it and tag you out of sheer gratitude.
- You have just acquired a new champion for your app without spending a single dollar on ads.

## Scaling This
Once the web app is live, change your DM script:
> *"Hey! I built a free tool that turns running photos into graphic design posters. I just generated one for you here [link]. You can play around with the templates if you want!"*
