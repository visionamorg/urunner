# Converting 2,300 Followers into Beta Testers

Instagram followers are a vanity metric if they don't actually download and test your application. You need to migrate them from Instagram into a private beta testing cohort.

## The Strategy: Artificial Scarcity

Runners are inherently competitive and love exclusive gear or early access.

### Step 1: The Waitlist Announcement
Put a Typeform or Google Form link in your Instagram bio. 
Post on your story: *"The alpha build of RunHub is finally compiling. The servers can only handle 50 runners right now. Link in bio to join the waitlist."*

### Step 2: The "Export Studio Exclusive" Hook
In your bio, state clearly what they get for joining:
*"Join the Beta to get early, free access to the Run Graphic Export Engine before it goes behind a paywall."*

### Step 3: The Drip Welcome
When people join the waitlist, don't just email them a download link. 
Email them personally: *"Hey, I'm the developer. Thanks for joining. Is there any specific feature you hate about your current running app that you want me to code into RunHub this weekend?"*

You now have a direct line to your most engaged followers, bypassing the Instagram algorithm completely. You have transformed passive scrollers into dedicated product testers who feel like they are building the app *with* you.
