# Instagram Story Cadence & Algorithm Training

You need to post on your Stories 3-5 times a day to maintain the top spot in the algorithmic feed of your 2,300 followers. Because you have the Export Studio, generating content is incredibly fast.

## The Daily Story Sequence

### 🔴 Morning (8:00 AM) - Personal Hustle
- **What:** A photo of your laptop screen showing your VS Code terminal running `npm start`.
- **Text:** "Coffee ☕ + Compiling the new route mapping engine for the app today."
- **Why:** Builds the authentic "developer journey" connection.

### 🟡 Mid-Day (12:30 PM) - The Community Feature
- **What:** Find a local runner. Run their stats through your Export Studio template.
- **Text:** "Crushing the midweek miles. 📍 Central Park. @username"
- **Why:** You get the "@tag" networking effect (see strategy 02) and showcase the core product.

### 🟢 Evening (6:00 PM) - The Question / Poll
- **What:** A screenshot of a UI decision or a new Export Template you are coding.
- **Text:** "Building out the new export layouts. Which one do you prefer?" + Add an Instagram Poll (Option A vs Option B).
- **Why:** Polls train the Instagram algorithm that your content is "engaging", pushing tomorrow's morning story to the very front of the line for your 2,300 followers.
