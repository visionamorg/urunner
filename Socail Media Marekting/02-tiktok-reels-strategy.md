# TikTok & Instagram Reels Strategy

Short-form algorithmic video is the fastest way to get your app in front of millions of runners for $0.

## The Strategy: Show, Don't Tell
No one wants to watch a screencast of a guy explaining a UI. They want aesthetic, highly satisfying content.

### Video Format 1: The "POV Run" Check-in
- **Visual:** A 7-second high-quality video of a beautiful trail or city street while running.
- **Overlay:** Use your own app's "Export Studio" aesthetic typography overlaying the video.
- **Audio:** Trending aesthetic TikTok audio.
- **Caption:** "Pov: discovering new routes. Tracked and mapped with RunHub."

### Video Format 2: The "Developer Challenge"
- **Visual:** You sitting at your desk looking stressed.
- **Text On Screen:** "Strava charges $8/month for this feature. I'm a developer, so I'm coding it for free in 24 hours."
- **Cut To:** Fast-lapse screen recording of you writing code (VS Code syntax highlighting looks cool).
- **Cut To:** The feature working on a phone.
- **Pyscology:** Taps into the tech trend and directly calls out a competitor frustration.

### Video Format 3: The 3D Map Visualization
- If you build the 3D Mapbox feature (epic16 in the export backlog), upload the raw output 10-second videos of crazy elevation routes (like running up a mountain) with dramatic music. Visually stunning maps always go viral.
