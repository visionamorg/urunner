# The User-Generated Content (UGC) Flywheel

The holy grail of marketing is when your users do the marketing for you. 
The **Activity Canvas & Export Studio** is designed entirely around this concept.

## The Cycle:
1. User goes for a run and records it on RunHub.
2. User wants to brag about their run on Instagram Stories, so they open the Export Studio.
3. User selects the "Typography Poster" template and exports it.
4. **The Catch:** The image contains a sleek, tasteful "Generated by UR Community / RunHub" logo and an invisible/aesthetic QR code.
5. User posts it to their Instagram Story (audience of 500 followers).
6. 10 of their friends are also runners, see the beautiful graphic, and ask "What app did you use to make that?" or they just scan the QR code.
7. Those 10 friends download RunHub.
8. The cycle repeats.

## Developer Action Items to Maximize UGC:
- **Don't make the watermark ugly.** If it's ugly, users will crop it out. Make it look like a designer watermark (e.g. `[ tracked 📍 on runhub.io ]`)
- **Add friction to removing it.** In the app, make the watermark default to ON. They have to toggle a setting deep in the privacy menu to turn it off. 90% of users won't bother.
- **Reward sharing.** Give users +50 "RunPoints" every time the iOS Share Sheet detects they pushed an image to the Instagram app bundle.
