# Epic: Garmin Clipboard - Pro Coaching Suite 📋🤝

## Goal
To build a professional-grade coaching environment within Runhub that mimics and enhances the "Garmin Clipboard" functionality. This suite will bridge the gap between athlete data and coach decision-making by providing deep visibility into Garmin-sourced performance and health metrics.

## Context
Running coaches currently have to toggle between multiple platforms to see their athletes' stress, sleep, and workout completion. By integrating Garmin's advanced Health and Activity APIs into a "Clipboard" view, Runhub becomes the central hub for team management.

## Core Pillars
1. **Unified Team Visibility**: Seeing all athlete data in one place without manual uploads.
2. **Predictive Readiness**: Surfacing HRV and Sleep scores to help coaches adjust plans proactively.
3. **Seamless Communication**: Allowing direct feedback on specific Garmin activity laps.
4. **Volume Management**: Pushing workouts from the coach's dashboard directly to the athlete's wrist.

## User Stories in this Epic
- **US-GC-001**: Athlete-Coach Linkage & Permission Hub.
- **US-GC-002**: The Unified Team Performance Feed.
- **US-GC-003**: Team Readiness Dashboard (HRV, Sleep, Stress).
- **US-GC-004**: Bulk Workout Push to Garmin Calendars (Coach Tool).
- **US-GC-005**: Coach Performance Analysis (Team CTL/ATL).
- **US-GC-006**: Coach-Athlete Garmin Feedback Hub.
