# Epic: Advanced Performance & Analytics
## Story: AI-Powered Dynamic Training Adjustments

**As a** user following a marathon training program,
**I want** the system to proactively adjust my upcoming runs if I miss a workout or overperform,
**So that** my plan stays relevant and I avoid overtraining or injury.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I skipped my Tuesday interval session, *when* the weekly AI cron job runs, *then* my Thursday run is automatically adjusted to a slightly harder interval session, and a notification explains why.
