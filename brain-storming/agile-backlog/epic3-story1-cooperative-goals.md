# Epic: Gamification and Challenges
## Story: Cooperative Community Distance Goals

**As a** member of a running community (e.g., "NYC Midnight Runners"),
**I want our** community to have a combined monthly distance goal (e.g., "Run to the Moon: 384,000km"),
**So that** my individual small runs feel like they contribute to a massive collective achievement.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* a community admin sets a cumulative 30-day goal, *when* any member logs a run, *then* the community progress bar advances.
- *When* the goal is reached, *then* all members who contributed receive a unique digital badge.
