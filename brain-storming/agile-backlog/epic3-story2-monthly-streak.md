# Epic: Gamification and Challenges
## Story: Monthly Streak Tracking

**As a** casual runner trying to build a habit,
**I want to** see a "Run Streak" counter on my dashboard,
**So that** I am motivated to get out the door at least a few times a week to keep the streak alive.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I ran 3 times this week, *then* my streak is active.
- *Given* I fail to run at least once in a 7-day rolling window, *then* the streak resets to zero.
