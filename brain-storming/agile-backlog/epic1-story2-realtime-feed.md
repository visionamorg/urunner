# Epic: Real-Time Social Engagement
## Story: Real-Time Feed Feedbacks (WebSockets)

**As an** active user,
**I want to** see likes and comments appear on my feed instantly without refreshing,
**So that** I can experience the immediate social validation and engagement of the app.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I am looking at my feed, *when* a friend likes my post from another device, *then* the like counter increments immediately via WebSocket broadcast.
