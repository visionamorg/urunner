# Epic: Brand Collaborations & Reward Points
## Story: Brand-Sponsored Challenges

**As a** brand partner (e.g., Nike, Salomon),
**I want to** host a sponsored community challenge (e.g., "The Salomon Trail 50k"),
**So that** I can build brand affinity, engage runners, and offer an exclusive reward to those who complete it.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I am a brand partner, *when* I launch a 30-day challenge, *then* it appears at the top of the app's "Challenges" tab with my brand logo.
- *Given* a user joins the challenge, *when* they complete 50km of trail running within 30 days, *then* they automatically unlock a unique digital badge and a 20% discount code for my e-commerce store.
