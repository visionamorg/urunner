# Epic: Advanced Social Dynamics
## Story: Pacesetter Groups & Follow the Leader

**As an** experienced marathon runner ("Pacer"),
**I want to** broadcast my scheduled weekend long run as a "Pace Group" (e.g., Target: 3h30m Marathon Pace),
**So that** other community members can join my specific group and run alongside me to hit their goals.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I am a verified Pacer, *when* I create an activity, *then* I can mark it as a "Pace Group" and set a precise target pace.
- *When* users join my group, *then* their apps sync to my location, and their UI updates to tell them if they are falling behind or pulling ahead of the Pacer.
