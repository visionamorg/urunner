# Epic: Brand Collaborations & Reward Points
## Story: Virtual Currency (RunPoints) Earning Mechanism

**As an** active user,
**I want to** earn "RunPoints" for every kilometer I run and for engaging with the community,
**So that** my real-world physical effort translates into tangible digital rewards.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I complete and upload a run, *when* the backend processes the activity, *then* I am awarded 10 RunPoints per kilometer.
- *Given* an anti-cheat mechanism, *when* my run exceeds physically possible paces (e.g., running 5km in 5 minutes), *then* the system flags the run and awards 0 points until manually reviewed.
- *When* I view my profile dashboard, *then* my total RunPoints balance is prominently displayed.
