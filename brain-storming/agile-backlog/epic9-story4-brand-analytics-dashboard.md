# Epic: Brand Collaborations & Reward Points
## Story: Brand Return-on-Investment (ROI) Dashboard

**As a** brand marketing manager,
**I want to** access an anonymized analytics dashboard for my sponsored challenges and marketplace rewards,
**So that** I can measure engagement, conversion rates, and the demographic reach of my campaign, ensuring a positive ROI.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* my brand is running a campaign, *when* I log into the Partner Portal, *then* I see metrics such as "Total Participants", "Total Kilometers Run for Brand", and "Codes Redeemed".
- *When* I view the demographic breakdown, *then* I only see anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., "55% Male, 45% Female, Age 25-34") to strictly comply with GDPR and privacy standards.
