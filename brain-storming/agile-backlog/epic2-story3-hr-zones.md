# Epic: Advanced Performance & Analytics
## Story: Heart Rate Zone Analysis

**As an** advanced runner with a Garmin/Apple Watch,
**I want to** view a breakdown of time spent in standard Heart Rate Zones (Z1-Z5),
**So that** I can ensure I am polarizing my training correctly (80/20 rule).

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* a run with HR timeseries data is imported, *then* an Angular Chart.js donut chart calculates and displays the exact minutes spent in each of the 5 zones.
