# Epic: Real-Time Social Engagement
## Story: Event Spectator Mode

**As a** friend of a runner,
**I want to** track their live GPS location during a virtual race and send live cheers,
**So that** I can support them even if I am not physically present.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* a runner is actively broadcasting their location in a virtual event, *then* their avatar moves on the live Mapbox/Leaflet map.
- *When* I click the "Cheer" button, *then* a floating emoji appears on the runner's screen.
