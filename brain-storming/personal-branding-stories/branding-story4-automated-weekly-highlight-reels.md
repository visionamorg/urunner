# Epic: Personal Branding & Growth Features
## Story: Automated Weekly Recap Highlight Reels

**As a** busy runner trying to build an audience,
**I want the** app to automatically stitch my 3 runs from this week into one 15-second vertical video,
**So that** I have instant content to post for "Sunday Runday" on Instagram Stories without editing it myself.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* it is Sunday at 5:00 PM, *then* a background job aggregates the user's distance and 3 photos from that week.
- *When* the user opens the app, *then* a "Your Weekly Highlight Reel is Ready" notification appears, offering a pre-rendered 9:16 video combining the photos, the total stats, and the RunHub watermark.
