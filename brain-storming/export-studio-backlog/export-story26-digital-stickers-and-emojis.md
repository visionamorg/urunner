# Epic: Activity Canvas & Export Studio
## Story: Advanced Builder: Digital Stickers & Badges

**As a** Gen-Z runner sharing to social media,
**I want to** open a sticker drawer and drag digital icons (e.g., Lightning Bolts, "PR!", Running Shoes, Coffee Cups) onto the canvas,
**So that** I can add personality and fun scrapbook-style flair to the export.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I am in the Export Builder, *when* I tap "Add Sticker", *then* a drawer opens with 20+ custom SVG graphics and emojis.
- *When* I select a sticker, it drops onto the canvas where I can pinch-to-zoom, rotate, and drag it into position before exporting.
