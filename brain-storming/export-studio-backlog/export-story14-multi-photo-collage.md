# Epic: Activity Canvas & Export Studio
## Story: Multi-Photo Collage Backgrounds

**As a** runner who took several great scenic photos during an ultra-marathon,
**I want to** select up to 3 photos to stitch into a collage background,
**So that** my single export graphic captures the full visual story of the trail.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I tap "Change Background", *when* I select multiple images (max 3), *then* the canvas splits the background into geometric collage blocks (e.g., vertical thirds or diagonal splits).
- *Then* the chosen data template seamlessly overlays across the merged collage background.

### Status: ✅ Done
**Implemented:** Multi-file upload (up to 3 photos) with collage layouts: "Vertical Thirds" (side-by-side panels) and "Diagonal" (diagonal clip-path splits). Layout picker in controls when multiple photos are uploaded. Preview thumbnails and clear button. Templates overlay seamlessly across the merged collage.
