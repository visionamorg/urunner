# Epic: Activity Canvas & Export Studio
## Story: Augmented Reality (AR) Medal Unlocks

**As an** achievement hunter who just finished a 100km virtual challenge,
**I want to** export my digital badge as a wearable Augmented Reality filter for Instagram/Snapchat,
**So that** I can take a selfie "wearing" the medal I just earned.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I unlock a major milestone badge, *when* I tap "Share via AR", *then* the app generates a Deep Link to a custom Instagram/Snapchat effect.
- *When* Instagram opens, *then* the AR effect tracks to my neck/chest and renders the 3D medal over my real-world selfie video.
