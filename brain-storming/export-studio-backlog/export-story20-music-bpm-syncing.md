# Epic: Activity Canvas & Export Studio
## Story: Peak-Pace Spotify Soundtrack Integration

**As a** runner motivated by music,
**I want the** export graphic to display the exact song I was listening to during my fastest kilometer,
**So that** I can share my "power song" with my followers.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* my RunHub account is linked to Spotify via OAuth, *when* the backend processes my finished 10K, *then* it cross-references the timestamp of my fastest 1km split with my Spotify listening history.
- *When* I open the Export Studio, *then* a specific mini-template widget embeds the Spotify Album Art and Track Name (e.g., "Powered by: Eye of the Tiger") into the layout.
