# Epic: Activity Canvas & Export Studio
## Story: "Explore Community Templates" Discovery Feed UI

**As a** creator or standard user looking for fresh export designs,
**I want a** dedicated "Explore" tab within the Export Studio that acts like a TikTok/Instagram feed for custom templates,
**So that** I can browse, preview, and instantly save incredible graphic templates designed by other athletes and running clubs.

**Status: Done**

### Acceptance Criteria:

1. **The Grid Layout:**
   - *Given* I click the "Explore Community" tab in the builder, *when* the page loads, *then* I am presented with a masonry grid (like Pinterest) of thousands of custom-made templates generated by other users.
   - *Then* the thumbnails are live previews, automatically utilizing *my own* most recent run data injected into the community creator's design layout.

2. **Categorization & Sorting algorithms:**
   - *Given* the Explore feed, *then* I can filter the grid using pill-shaped tags (e.g., "Minimalist", "Editorial", "Ultra-Running", "Club Branded").
   - *Then* the feed supports sorting by "Trending This Week" (based on export volume) and "Highest Rated" (based on community upvotes/saves).

3. **Creator Attribution & Follow:**
   - *Given* I hover or tap on a template in the grid, *then* it displays the username and avatar of the developer/designer who created it.
   - *When* I tap their username, *then* it routes me to their public profile where I can "Follow" them to get pinged whenever they publish a new template.

4. **One-Tap "Save to My Templates":**
   - *Given* I find a cyberpunk layout I love, *when* I tap the "Save to Library" (bookmark) icon, *then* that template is instantly added to my personal quick-access drawer when I export my next run.
   - *Then* the creator receives a push notification ("Alice saved your Cyberpunk template!").
