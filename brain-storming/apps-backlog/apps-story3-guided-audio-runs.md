# Epic: Nike Run Club / Apps Baseline
## Story: Guided Audio Runs

**As a** beginner runner,
**I want to** download and listen to pre-recorded coaching sessions mixed with my Spotify music,
**So that** an elite coach or celebrity can guide my breathing, form, and pacing throughout a 30-minute run.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I browse the "Guided Runs" library, *when* I download "First 5K" and press play, *then* the audio plays a voiceover track in the foreground while automatically ducking the volume of my background music app (Spotify/Apple Music).
