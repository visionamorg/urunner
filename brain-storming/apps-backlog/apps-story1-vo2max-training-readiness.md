# Epic: Garmin & Fitness Apps Baseline
## Story: VO2 Max & Training Readiness

**As a** data-focused athlete,
**I want to** see an estimated VO2 Max and a daily "Training Readiness" score (0-100),
**So that** I know exactly how fit I am and whether I should push hard today or take a recovery day.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I complete a run featuring GPS pace and Heart Rate data, *then* the app runs an algorithm to estimate my VO2 Max.
- *Given* my sleep data and recent relative effort history, *when* I open the app each morning, *then* I receive a Training Readiness score (e.g., "75 - Prime for a hard workout").
