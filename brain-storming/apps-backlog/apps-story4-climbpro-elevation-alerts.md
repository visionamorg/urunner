# Epic: Garmin & Fitness Apps Baseline
## Story: ClimbPro (Live Climb Tracker)

**As a** trail or mountain runner,
**I want to** see a live profile of the specific climb I am currently suffering through,
**So that** I can manage my effort by knowing exactly how much elevation gain and distance is left before the summit.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I am navigating a pre-loaded route, *when* I approach a categorized climb, *then* a specific "Climb" screen pops up showing a color-coded gradient map of *just* the current hill, displaying "Distance to Summit" and "Grade %".
