# Epic: Garmin & Fitness Apps Baseline
## Story: Live GroupTrail Tracking (GroupTrack)

**As a** group runner or cyclist,
**I want to** see the live locations of up to 50 of my friends on the map at the exact same time during a group ride/run,
**So that** I can see who got dropped, who is surging ahead, and regroup effectively.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* a group of friends starts a session simultaneously in an active "GroupTrack" channel, *when* I look at my map screen, *then* I see little floating avatars for each friend moving in real-time.
