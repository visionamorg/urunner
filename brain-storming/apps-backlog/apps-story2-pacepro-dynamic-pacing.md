# Epic: Garmin & Fitness Apps Baseline
## Story: PacePro (Dynamic Race Pacing Tool)

**As a** marathoner,
**I want to** input a GPX route and a target finish time, and receive an exact split-by-split pacing strategy,
**So that** I know exactly what pace to hit on uphill miles vs downhill miles to achieve my goal.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I upload a hilly marathon GPX file and select a 3:30:00 goal, *when* I tap "Generate PacePro", *then* the app outputs a custom pacing chart where uphill kilometers are slightly slower and downhills are faster to average the required overall pace.
