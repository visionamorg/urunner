# Epic: Garmin & Fitness Apps Baseline
## Story: Daily Suggested Workouts

**As a** runner without a structured 12-week plan,
**I want the** app to suggest exactly one running workout every morning when I wake up,
**So that** my daily training is balanced (e.g., suggesting a sprint session if I haven't done speedwork, or a recovery jog if my HR is high).

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I do not have a hardcoded training program active, *when* I open the app on Tuesday, *then* it displays a "Daily Suggestion: 45min Base Run @ 6:00/km" based on my current training load.
