# Epic: Strava Baseline Features
## Story: "Kudos" and Rich Social Interactions

**As an** active community participant,
**I want to** easily give "Kudos" (a quick thumbs up) and mention other users in the descriptions of my runs,
**So that** I can encourage my friends and document who I ran with.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I view a friend's feed, *when* I tap the big orange Kudos icon, *then* the count increments immediately and sends a push notification.
- *Given* I am editing my run description, *when* I type `@nickname`, *then* an autocomplete dropdown appears, linking their profile to my activity.
