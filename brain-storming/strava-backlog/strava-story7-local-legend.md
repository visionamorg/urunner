# Epic: Strava Baseline Features
## Story: Local Legend Status

**As a** consistent runner,
**I want to** earn a "Local Legend" laurel wreath if I run a specific segment more times than anyone else in a 90-day rolling window,
**So that** I can earn recognition for sheer volume and consistency, rather than just raw speed.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I run "Main Street Hill" 15 times in the last 90 days (the most of any user), *when* I view the segment leaderboard, *then* a laurel wreath icon appears next to my name declaring me the Local Legend.
