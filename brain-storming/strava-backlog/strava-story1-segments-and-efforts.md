# Epic: Strava Baseline Features
## Story: Segments & Leaderboards

**As a** competitive runner,
**I want to** define and compete on specific geographic sections of road/trail ("Segments"),
**So that** I can fight for the top spot (KOM/QOM) on that specific stretch without running an entire race.

### Acceptance Criteria:
- *Given* I finish a run traversing a known segment polygon, *when* the activity is saved, *then* my split time for that segment is automatically calculated and placed on the All-Time, Yearly, and Following leaderboards.
