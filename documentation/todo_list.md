# RunHub Backlog & Roadmap (TODO List)

The following list outlines the planned features and technical improvements to transform RunHub into a professional-grade, social-first ecosystem for athletes.

## 🛠️ Pillar 1: Technical Excellence (Foundations)
- [ ] **API Documentation (Swagger/OpenAPI)**: Comprehensive, interactive documentation for external developers and AI tools.
- [ ] **Automated Testing Suite**: Implementation of Unit, Integration, and E2E tests for both backend and frontend layers.
- [ ] **Global Semantic Search**: A unified search experience using AI to find people, communities, events, and activities from one interface.
- [ ] **OWASP Security Protocols**: Hardening the platform with advanced rate-limiting, secure headers, and vulnerability scanning.
- [ ] **Internationalization (i18n)**: Multi-language support, focusing on English, French, and Arabic to support the Moroccan running community.

## 📊 Pillar 2: Elite Athlete Insights (AI & Science)
- [ ] **Elite Performance Dashboard (PMC)**: Implementation of the Performance Management Chart (Fitness, Fatigue, Form) based on professional training standards.
- [ ] **AI-Driven Voice Assistant**: Hands-free interactions using voice commands during active runs.
- [ ] **Weather-Adaptive Smart Pacing**: Real-time pacing adjustments based on temperature, humidity, and wind conditions.
- [ ] **Shoe Lifecycle Tracker**: Monitoring mileage per pair of shoes with automated notifications for replacement.
- [ ] **Nutrition & Hydration Integration**: Tracking fuel intake and providing AI-guided suggestions based on activity intensity.

## 🏆 Pillar 3: Viral Social & Gamification
- [ ] **Real-time Audio Kudos (Vibe Cheers)**: Enabling friends to send audio messages or cheers that play instantly in a runner's headphones.
- [ ] **Community Challenges & Rewards**: Automated team-based competitions and individual leaderboard challenges with virtual/tangible rewards.
- [ ] **Streak Safeguard**: "Streak Freezes" to protect a user's consistency metrics during injury or illness.
- [ ] **3D Digital Collectibles**: Reward runners with unique digital assets (Collectibles) for record-breaking performances or event completions.

## 🛡️ Pillar 4: Safety & Privacy
- [ ] **Privacy Stealth Zones**: Geofencing tools to automatically hide the start and end points of runs (near home or work) from public maps.
- [ ] **Emergency Live Tracking**: Real-time location sharing with trusted contacts specifically during active sessions.

## 🔗 Ecosystem & Integrations
- [ ] **Garmin OAuth Synchronization**: Direct health and activity data sync from Garmin Connect.
- [ ] **Strava Sync Stabilization**: Resolving activity duplication and ensuring reliable background syncing.
- [ ] **Stripe Payment Gateway**: 
    - **Community Store**: Selling merchandise and training gear.
    - **Premium Enrollment**: Paid elite training programs.
    - **Event Ticketing**: Secure registration for marathons and local runs.
- [ ] **Native Hardware Extensions**: Developing native applications or watch faces for Garmin and Apple Watch.

## 📢 Content & Growth
- [ ] **Advanced Export Studio**: Moving beyond images to dynamic video recaps and Reels-ready content for Instagram/TikTok.
- [ ] **Weekly Agenda Generator**: Automatically generating stylized community schedule images for social media promotion.
- [ ] **Referral Rewards System**: Incentivizing current members to invite new runners to the platform.
